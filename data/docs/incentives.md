# Dealer Incentive Program 2026

## Tiering System
Dealers are categorized based on quarterly purchase volume and churn risk score.

### 1. High Value (Gold Tier)
-   **Criteria**: > 100 units/quarter OR Volume > â‚¬1.5M
-   **Benefits**:
    -   2.5% rebate on all purchases.
    -   Priority access to "Fresh Inventory" (first 24 hours).
    -   Dedicated Account Manager.
    -   Extended payment terms (Net 60).

### 2. Standard (Silver Tier)
-   **Criteria**: 20-99 units/quarter
-   **Benefits**:
    -   1.0% rebate if target reached.
    -   Standard payment terms (Net 30).

### 3. At Risk / Probation
-   **Criteria**: High Churn Score (> 0.7) OR < 20 units/quarter
-   **Restrictions**:
    -   Pre-payment required.
    -   No rebates applicable.
    -   Access restricted to "Aged Inventory" (> 60 days) if score > 0.9.

## Volume Boost Bonus
Any dealer increasing Q-over-Q volume by 20% receives an additional 0.5% bonus on the incremental volume.
