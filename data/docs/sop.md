# Internal Sales SOP: Rep Guidelines

## Lead Response SLA
-   **Website Leads (Hot)**: Must be contacted within **15 minutes**. Conversion probability drops by 50% after 1 hour.
-   **Referrals**: Contact within 4 hours.
-   **Email Inquiries**: Response required within 24 hours.

## Discount Authority
-   **Sales Rep**: Up to 3% discretionary discount on vehicles aged > 30 days.
-   **Senior Rep**: Up to 5%.
-   **Sales Manager**: Up to 10% (requires written approval).
-   **VP Sales**: > 10% (Strategic Accounts only).

## Rules of Engagement
-   Do not poach leads assigned to another rep (check `assigned_rep_id`).
-   Update lead status to "Contacted" immediately after first call.
-   "Lost" leads must have a reason code (Price, Stock, Competitor).
